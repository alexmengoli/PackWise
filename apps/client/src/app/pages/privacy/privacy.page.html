1. Who this policy covers

This Privacy Policy explains how Packwise handles information in its web, Progressive Web App, and Android versions. Packwise is developed and published by Alex Mengoli, operating as MxApps (collectively, “Packwise,” “MxApps,” “we,” or “us”).

This policy covers the current offline-first version of Packwise. Planned account and cloud-sync controls shown in the app are disabled and are not part of the current service. This policy will be updated before those features begin processing data.

2. Data handled by Packwise

Content you create

Packwise lets you create and store:

  • activity names, notes, colors, and icons;
  • item names, notes, categories, mandatory-item settings, and activity associations;
  • trip names, notes, selected activities, and packed-item status; and
  • locally generated record identifiers and creation or modification timestamps.

This content is not inherently account information, but it may contain personal or sensitive details if you choose to enter them. Please avoid putting information in item, activity, or trip notes that you do not want stored on the device or included in an export.

What the app does not collect

The current app does not create user profiles and does not collect or transmit your packing content, identity, email address, precise or approximate location, contacts, photos, camera or microphone data, advertising identifiers, payment information, analytics, diagnostics, or usage history to Packwise or MxApps. It does not use ads, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics SDKs.

3. Local storage and device backups

Packwise uses storage provided by your browser or operating system:

  • IndexedDB stores activities, items, trips, notes, and packed-item state.
  • Local and session storage remember the active trip and temporary, unsaved checklist selections using locally generated record identifiers.
  • Service-worker and browser caches store app files such as code, fonts, and icons so the web app can work offline. Packing content is not intentionally placed in the service-worker asset cache.

Storage is scoped to Packwise by the browser or Android app sandbox. Other people who can access your unlocked device or browser profile may still be able to see your data.

On Android, the operating system may include Packwise app data in a device backup when system backup is enabled. Whether this happens, where the backup is stored, and how long it is retained depend on your device, operating-system settings, and backup provider. MxApps does not receive or control those backups.

4. Import, export, and sharing

Import and export happen only when you request them. An export is a readable JSON file containing your activities, items, trips, notes, associations, packed status, local identifiers, timestamps, and the export time. Treat it like any other personal backup file.

In a web browser, Packwise creates a local download. On Android, Packwise writes a temporary copy to the app cache and opens the operating system share sheet. The app you select, the destination you choose, and any storage or communication provider involved will handle the file under their own terms and privacy policy. Packwise does not choose a recipient or upload an export automatically.

Import reads only the JSON file you select and replaces the current Packwise database after confirmation. Packwise does not scan unrelated files or request broad access to your device storage.

5. Web hosting data

The public web version of Packwise is hosted with GitHub Pages. When you visit that version, your browser connects to GitHub to download the app and check for updates. GitHub states that it logs visitors’ IP addresses for security and may process standard request and usage information as an independent service provider. This hosting data is separate from the packing content held in your browser.

MxApps does not add analytics or tracking to the hosted app and does not receive your packing database from GitHub Pages. For GitHub’s own practices, see the GitHub General Privacy Statement.

The installed Android app is packaged with its app files and does not require a Packwise server or account for its core features.

6. Third parties and disclosure

Packwise does not sell or rent personal data. It does not share your locally stored packing content with MxApps, advertisers, data brokers, analytics providers, or other third parties.

Third parties may process information only in these situations:

  • GitHub processes technical hosting data when you use the GitHub Pages web version.
  • Your operating system or chosen destination processes an export when you save or share it.
  • Your device backup provider may process app data if device backup is enabled.
  • A service you choose to contact MxApps may process the information you voluntarily provide in your inquiry.

If you contact MxApps, we use the contact details and message you provide to respond, troubleshoot, protect the service, or meet legal obligations. We do not combine support messages with your on-device packing data.

7. Security

Packwise reduces privacy risk by keeping its main data store local, avoiding accounts and tracking, using browser and Android app isolation, validating imported files, and serving the hosted web app over HTTPS. However, no device, browser, file, or storage method is completely secure.

Packwise does not separately encrypt the local database or exported JSON files. Protect your device with an appropriate screen lock, keep your browser and operating system updated, and store exports only in locations you trust. Anyone who obtains an exported JSON file can read its contents.

8. Retention and deletion

Packing content stays on your device until you edit it, delete individual records, import replacement data, choose Delete all data in Settings, clear Packwise site/app storage, or uninstall the app. Temporary session state normally lasts for the current browser tab or session. Cached app files may remain until your browser or operating system clears them.

“Delete all data” clears the activities, items, and saved trips in the main local database. Small local state keys may remain until site/app storage is cleared; these contain only locally generated identifiers and no item, activity, trip, or note text. Copies you previously exported, files handled by another app, and system device backups must be deleted through the location or provider where they are stored.

Because MxApps does not receive your packing database, we cannot access, recover, or remotely delete it. If you contact us, we retain the inquiry only as long as reasonably necessary to respond, keep appropriate support records, protect the service, and comply with law.

9. Your choices and rights

You control your Packwise content directly. You can:

  • review and edit activities, items, trips, notes, and packed status in the app;
  • export a copy in JSON format;
  • delete individual records or all local data from Settings;
  • clear Packwise storage using your browser or operating-system controls; and
  • disable or manage device backups in your operating-system settings.

Depending on where you live, privacy law may also give you rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, or objection concerning personal data a controller holds about you, and to lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority. MxApps generally has no copy of your on-device content, so those requests are most effectively completed using the controls above. You may contact us about any personal information contained in a support inquiry.

Requests about technical information processed independently by GitHub or a backup/share provider should be directed to that provider.

10. Children's privacy

Packwise is a general-audience utility and is not directed to children under 13. The app does not knowingly collect children’s personal information for MxApps. A parent or guardian who believes a child sent personal information to MxApps may contact us to request its deletion. On-device content can be removed with the app and device controls described above.

11. Changes and contact

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when Packwise features, distribution methods, or legal requirements change. The effective date at the top will be revised. If a future feature changes how the app handles data—for example, optional accounts or cloud sync—we will update this policy and provide any notice or choice required before that processing begins.
